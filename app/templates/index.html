Welcome to HIPE

the game of HIPE was described by Peter Winkler in his excellent collection of mathematical puzzles

the rules are very straightforward, you are given a string of letters, and your goal is to find an English word that contains those letters as a substring. So, for example, if the string were 'HIPE', the answer might be 'arcHIPElago'.
